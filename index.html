Frequently Asked Questions

Hulu is an American subscription streaming service that offers a wide variety of television shows, movies, and original content. It is majority-owned by The Walt Disney Company, with Comcast holding a minority stake through NBC Universal.

Hulu allows users to watch a wide range of content on various devices, including computers, smartphones, tablets, and smart TVs. The platform provides a combination of current-season TV episodes, classic TV shows, movies, and exclusive content produced by Hulu itself. Users can choose between different subscription plans, which may include options with or without ads.

Hulu offers several different plans, each with its own monthly price. Here is a breakdown of Hulu's current plans and pricing:

  • Hulu (With Ads): This is Hulu's basic plan and costs $7.99 per month. It includes access to Hulu's entire streaming library, but you will have to watch ads.
  • Hulu (No Ads): This plan is the same as the basic plan, but without the ads. It costs $14.99 per month.
  • Hulu + Live TV: This plan includes everything in the No Ads plan, plus over 75 live channels. It costs $64.99 per month.
  • Hulu, Disney+, and ESPN+: This bundle includes all three streaming services for a discounted price of $13.99 per month.

You can cancel your Hulu subscription by following these steps:


On a computer:

  1. Go to hulu.com and log in to your account.
  2. Click on your profile picture in the top right corner of the screen.
  3. Select Account.
  4. Scroll down to the Your Subscription section and click on Cancel.
  5. Click on Continue to Cancel.
  6. Select Cancel Subscription.

On a mobile device:

  1. Open the Hulu app and log in to your account.
  2. Tap on your profile picture in the top right corner of the screen.
  3. Select Account.
  4. Tap on Cancel.
  5. Tap on Continue to Cancel.
  6. Select Cancel Subscription.
